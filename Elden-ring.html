Elden Ring

About The Game

Elden Ring is a 2022 action role-playing game called Elden Ring was created by FromSoftware and released by Bandai Namco Entertainment. It was directed by Hidetaka Miyazaki with worldbuilding provided by fantasy writer George R. R. Martin. The game allows the players to control a character that may be customized as they travel to fix the shattered Elden Ring and take over as the new Elden Lord. Players are able to explore the game's dynamic open environment while viewing it from a third-person perspective. Horseback riding, crafting, and battle utilizing a variety of weapons and magic spells are all included in the gameplay. Elden Ring is a master piece, we rate it 10/10 and recommend everyone to play it. It won Game Of The Year award, what more do you want us to say.

Story

The world setting of Elden Ring takes place in the Lands Between, a made-up continent governed by a number of demigods. The immortal Queen Marika, who served as the guardian of the Elden Ring, a powerful force that took the form of the physical notion of order, had previously been in charge of it. Following Marika's eventual destruction of the Elden Ring and disappearance, a conflict known as the Shattering erupted between her demigod children for fragments of the Ring. Each demigod carries a Great Rune, a piece of the Ring that has the power to corrupt them. In the game, the player character is a Tarnished, one of a group of exiles from the Lands Between who are summoned back after the Shattering.
